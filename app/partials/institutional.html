The Company

Medlaser US was developed for providing high laser technology, meeting both the spinal and vascular segments. The name Medlaser was created from the joining of the words laser and medicine, which emphasize our work. Among other products distributed by Medlaser, we highlight the brand NEOLASER, a company which specializes in laser technology, known worldwide for its continuous pursue of evolving technologies and products. Medlaser has been investing in the deployment of new products and technologies. We have the NeoV machine, which is a powerful and versatile medical laser, utilizing a breakthrough cooling design, enabling significant power delivery, in the smallest footprint and lightest weight unit available on the market. THE NEOV – WORLD CLASS DESIGN, PERFORMANCE AND VALUE.
