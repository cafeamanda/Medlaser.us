The Optimal Tool for Endovenous Treatment

The Optimal Energy – 1470nm

The neoV 1470 diode laser offers a wavelength with superior performance for ablation of diseased veins – 1470 nm. In contrast to previous generations of laser therapy with deep tissue penetration, the 1470nm wavelength is highly absorbed by water in tissue. As a result heat generation is highly localized and application is safe and precise.
