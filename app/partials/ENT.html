Otology

The neoV laser offers a delicate and high precision modality for inner ear treatments. By use of a special design small core fiber disposable kit, fine ablation and tissue removal can be achieved. The use of a hand-held fiber delivery system with a clearly visible aiming beam ensures safe application and high precision control of energy application.

Neolaser offers a flexible reusable hand piece system consisting of a rigid ergonomic handpiece and various termination cannulas, or a fully disposable sterile handpiece with an integrated high precision fiber.

Fiber Delivery & Accessories

Neolaser offers a wide and comprehensive set of surgical accessories for multiple specialties. Neolaser's modular surgical hand piece system allows you to pick and choose the size and shape of cannula matching the treated anatomy. Simply plug your cannula of choice on the ergonomic hand piece for easy access. Custom tubes can be designed upon request. Additional specialty hand pieces are also available, such as the laryngeal hand pieces and the suction hand piece.

Benefits

Minimally Invasive

The neoV platform enables a wide variety of minimally invasive treatments. The ability to deliver high power energy through a flexible small diameter fiber facilitates and expands many treatment options.

Multi Platform

Given the flexibility of fiber delivery options, as well as available surgical hand pieces, the neoV laser can be used in conjunction with every surgical platform and approach. From hand held instruments under surgical microscope visualization, through endoscopic approaches, whether rigid or flexible, including laparoscopy, to robotic channels, all options are available for safe and effective energy delivery.

Various Wavelengths

The neoV980 and neoV1470 offer two highly effective yet different tools for your ENT practice, driven by their different absorption in soft tissue. Accordingly, both systems cover wide options of tissue interaction, from strong and deep coagulation, to precision micro-surgical excisions.

Flexible Tissue Interaction

The energy, delivered through solid core fiber optics, enables work in both contact and non contact modes. Contact work provides tactile feedback and rapid cutting, while non contact mode enables vaporization and coagulation of large areas.

Multi Fiber Options

The neoV lasers can be delivered through a wide selection of fibers, with cores ranging from 300 to 600 microns, and with multiple options of beam emission from the tip.

Office Procedures

Given the small size, yet high power of the neoV platform, it is the perfect tool to allow shift of procedures from the OR to an office-based setting. You can depend that it will provide you with enough power to tackle challenging procedures in a safe and effective manner.
